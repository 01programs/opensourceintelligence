Read first:

This website is dedicated to help people find and gather information from open sources more easily. For now this list focuses on WWW ressources. For the future it is planned to include offline ressources, processes, handbooks, etc as well. This website is a WIP and your contributions and additions are very welcome. Please note: There is a difference between open source and free and open source. Open in this case is defined as not restricted to a group of people (with eg. special clearance). This does not mean that all of the ressources are necessarily free to use.


Contribute @: https://github.com/01programs/opensourceintelligence
